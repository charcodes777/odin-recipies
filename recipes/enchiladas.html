Enchiladas

So cheesy!

This chicken enchilada recipe couldnâ€™t be easier to make with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • salsa
  • enchilada seasoning
  • cheese
  • chopped tomatoes
  • tortillas
  • onion
  • peppers

Steps

  1. Fry onion and peppers
  2. Add chicken
  3. Combine chopped tomatoes and salsa
  4. Add half the tomato mix over chicken
  5. Line baking dish with tortillas, spoon on chicken cover with tortillas
  6. Cover in rest of tomato mix, add cheese, bake in oven for 35 mins at 200c

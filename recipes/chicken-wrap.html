Chicken Wrap

Quick and easy!

The perfect lunch when you're on the go!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • You choice of sauce
  • Tortilla wraps

Steps

  1. Airfry chicken tenders
  2. Assemble wrap with lettuce cucumber and sauce
  3. Add cooked chicken and wrap!

Beef Noodles

Ready in 30 mins!

Quick, tasty and it uses your storecupboard basics. A perfect mid-week meal

Ingredients

  • 3 blocks egg noodles
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 400g pack beef stir-fry strips
  • Stir fry sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to pack instructions. A minute before they are ready, tip in broccoli.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a wok until very hot, then stir-fry the beef for 2-3 mins until well browned.
  3. Tip in the sauce, give it a stir, let it simmer for a moment, then turn off the heat.
  4. Drain the noodles, stir into the beef and serve straight away, scattered with spring onions.
